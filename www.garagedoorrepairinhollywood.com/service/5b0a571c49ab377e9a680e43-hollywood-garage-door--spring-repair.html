Garage door spring repair

So basically there are 2 types of garage door springs: torsion springs are mounted on a metal bar above the door and extension springs are suspended overhead above tracks on a pulley system. Hypothetically you can adjust extension spring yourself. Just move the hook to a different hole and don’t dismantle the spring. But if you adjust the spring the wrong way, it will get worse. It’s even more dangerous to adjust torsion springs because they are under tension. You must have a thorough understanding how this kind of spring works and have certain skills. It’s definitely easier to call Near Me Services and let them fix the problem.

WARNING –Remember, garage door springs, cables, pulleys, brackets, and other hardware attached to the springs are under extreme tension. Adjusting them can cause serious injury. Only a qualified professional should adjust them by carefully following the manufacturer's instructions.
