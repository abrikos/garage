Hi guys, I’m Matt, I’m a tow truck driver working in Glendale. A lot of people don’t like tow truck drivers because when they get towed, they have to pay for it, and they might even have to pay to get the car out of the impound lot. Well, guys, that’s easy to avoid, if you don’t want to get towed, don’t park in front of the ‘NO PARKING’ sign. If it’s posted no parking, private parking only or tow away zone, then you shouldn’t park there, not even “for a minute”. I know it’s tempting, but you must know, it takes less than a minute to tow a car (well, it takes me less than 30 seconds). So even if it’s “only for a minute” that’s still plenty of time for me to do my job! But it’s not always about towing illegally parked cars; there are people who are even glad to meet me. Sometimes I do gas delivery, roadside assistance and winch out services. I also have experience with motorcycle towing, truck towing, SUV towing and 4 wheel drive towing. In fact, just recently I got a call from someone who had the worst luck on the road! He was driving on the freeway when he got a flat tire. Being a handy guy, he changed the tire. But just 20 minutes after he changed his flat, the rear tire on the passenger side blew out! Of course, by that time, he had to call for a tow. I arrived and was even able to use my self-loading wheel dolly and towed his car to get some new tires. Well, I’ve got to head out on another call, I’ll write more when I have got spare time. Drive save everyone!
