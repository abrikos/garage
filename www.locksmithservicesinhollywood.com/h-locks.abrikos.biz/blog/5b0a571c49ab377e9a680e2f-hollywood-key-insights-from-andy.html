Hey, Dan here, I'm a locksmith working and living in Hollywood. You know, most of my acquaintances have a regular life, they get up early, go to work, sit in their offices, then go home, and sit on the couch and hate the day they spent sitting in their offices. They don’t think much about their jobs. But it’s different for me. I know, some people think I’m just a locksmith. But I’m more than that, I'm an "Emergency Locksmith", a "Security Consultant", a "Residential Locksmith", a "Car Locksmith", an "Opener of Doors" and, sometimes even a "Superhero". I’ll tell you why; from time to time, I get the opportunity to help clients who've moved into a new home choose the best locks and help them feel safe and secure. Some days, I can swoop in to save the day by helping some people with their own emergency. For instance, last month, I got a call from a stressed mother. She went grocery shopping with her 2 year-old boy and then tried to load her groceries and her child into the van, of course, it wasn’t that easy, the boy was bored and demanded attention, so she wanted to distract him for a couple of minutes, so she gave him the first thing she had in her hand, the car keys, to play with. So Mom put the groceries in the trunk and found out that the boy locked the car from the inside. After ‘ensuring his safety’ he tossed the keys away. The poor woman felt stressed because the baby was crying in the car and foolish because she caused all this drama herself. She was so relieved to see me and when I opened the car and saw her hugging her child, I felt like Superman. So don’t hesitate, give us a call.
