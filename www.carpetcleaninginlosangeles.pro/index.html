Regardless of what people say, carpets and rugs are very comfortable!

It’s always cozier and warmer in a house with carpets or rugs. In fact, it’s much warmer. After all, it’s much more comfortable to walk on a carpeted floor than on cold stone, tiles or wood. But the trouble is that they collect dust incredibly quickly. It would seem, only yesterday this carpet was vacuumed, and today it’s already covered with dirt, fluff and ubiquitous dust. Ah, this isn’t fair! Carpet cleaning is one of the most tedious and ungrateful chores because we must do it constantly. And you can't get away from it. If you like to have carpets or rugs around the house, you just need to learn to take care of them. Let's start with the fact that because of inept handling they quickly lose their original appearance and bright color, they fade and wear out. But a good carpet or rug made of natural wool is not so cheap, so it’s a waste to mistreat it and then throw it away. In order to keep them in good condition, to extend their useful life, it is necessary to follow some rules of carpet and rug care. Best of all, of course, is a professional carpet cleaning.

Carpet cleaning at home:

stages
In order not to ruin your favorite carpet, but at the same time to remove all contamination and persistent odor 100%, we must choose the right reagents and their concentrations. Our staff are experts at deciding the correct combinations; therefore, we guarantee that we use means suitable for a given type of a material. All chemicals have been tested and are safe for humans and animals.
The cleaning procedure for carpets and upholstery has five phases:

01.Dry cleaning

A powerful cleaner removes dust and fresh contaminants that have not "settled" in the fiber.

02.Dissolving old dirt

The application of a special foam solution that is water-based dissolves old dirt.

03.Cleaning of reagents

Cleaning foam is applied with professional equipment and traces of reagents are removed completely.

04.Manual cleaning of carpets, rugs and upholstery

Unfortunately, the spot that is more than a week old is almost impossible to remove with the reagent; therefore, old dirt and stains are eliminated manually.

05.Drying

The carpet must be dried very carefully in order to restore the structure of the fibers and eliminate the risk of mold and fungi.

Risks of unprofessional cleaning

A powerful vacuum cleaner is able to remove about 60% of dust, but the remaining 40% penetrate deep into the fibers and form a ‘risk zone’. The ‘risk zone’ provides a breeding ground for dust mites, which are sources of allergens. Over time, the use of these carpets might lead to health problems, and wet cleaning with household chemicals only aggravates the situation.

Rescue don’t ruin

“What doesn’t kill you makes you stronger” works for dirt in the carpet or rug too. Using detergents, inexperienced cleaners "push" the dirt deeper and make it impossible to clean it at home. Some veteran stains need professional cleaning only.

One confused kitty

One more risk is odor. Some odors are imperceptible to the human sense of smell, but they're attractive for pets: a cat can "confuse" a carpet with a litter box and start using the expensive rug as its regular place, which makes for a cute video and an unhappy rug owner. Of course, this can lead to a threat of infection, especially if kids walk barefoot on the floor.

Carpet cleaning service will save carpets from early retirement to the trash, restoring their structure damaged from long-term use and preserving the brightness of colors.
