Hey everyone, I’m Bill,

Hey everyone, I’m Bill, I’m a tow truck driver working in LA. You know, a lot of people don’t like tow truck drivers. My friends are always complaining that when you get towed, you have to pay for it, and you might even have to pay to get your car out of the impound lot. Well, here’s what I always say “If you don’t want to get towed, don’t park in front of the ‘NO PARKING’ sign!” If it’s posted no parking, private parking only or tow away zone, then you shouldn’t park there, not even “for a few minutes”. I know it’s tempting, you better believe that I feel tempted too, but remember, it takes less than 60 seconds to tow a car (unless you’re me, then it takes less than 20). So even if it’s “only for a minute” that’s still too long! But don’t get me wrong, it’s not all about finding and towing illegally parked cars, some people are even happy to see me coming. I also do gas delivery, roadside assistance and winch out services. I can, and have experience with just about anything, including motorcycle towing, truck towing, SUV towing and 4 wheel drive towing. In fact, just last week, I got a call from someone who had the worst luck I’ve ever heard of! He was driving on the 101 when he got a flat. Being a handy guy, he was able to do the tire change himself. But you’ll never believe it, just 15 minutes after he changed his flat, the rear tire on the passenger side blew out! Of course, by that time, he had to call a for a tow. I arrived and was even able to use my self-loading wheel dolly and towed his car to his mechanic’s shop to get some new tires. Well, I’ve got to head out on another call, I’ll write more later. Drive save everyone, even though I’m experienced with accident recovery and wrecker service, I’d rather not see any of you nice people have that kind of problem!
