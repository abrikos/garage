I love classic and antique cars. I have a modest collection (3 cars), and I love to go to car shows and bring one or two of my cars with me. Since I’m only one person, I can’t drive two cars, but I always call Near Me Service for vehicle transportation. The best part is that they have experience with motorcycle towing, flatbed towing, exotic vehicle towing and luxury vehicle towing. They treat my cars with kid gloves and make sure that they arrive there and back in pristine condition. This way, I not only get to take two cars to the show, but I also get to reduce mileage and wear and tear by only driving one to the show.
